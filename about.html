About Grand Paradise Hotel

Our Story

Grand Paradise Hotel has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality for over three decades. Founded in 1992 by the visionary entrepreneur James Wellington, our hotel has consistently set the standard for excellence in the hospitality industry. What began as a dream to create an extraordinary guest experience has evolved into one of the most prestigious hotels in the region.

Our Mission

At Grand Paradise Hotel, our mission is to provide an unparalleled luxury experience that exceeds every guest's expectations. We are committed to delivering exceptional service, maintaining the highest standards of quality, and creating memorable moments that last a lifetime. Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every aspect of your stay reflects our commitment to excellence.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for perfection in every detail, from our pristine accommodations to our impeccable service standards.

Our Facilities

Grand Paradise Hotel features world-class amenities designed to cater to every aspect of your stay. Our facilities include 200 elegantly appointed guest rooms and suites, three award-winning restaurants, a state-of-the-art fitness center, a luxurious spa, two swimming pools, conference and event spaces, and a business center. Every facility is meticulously maintained and equipped with modern amenities to ensure your comfort and convenience.
